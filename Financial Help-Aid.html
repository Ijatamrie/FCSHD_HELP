Budget-Friendly 

Tips for FCSHD Students

At UNIMAS, students can manage their budgets effectively while enjoying their campus life. Here are some tips to help you minimize your expenses:

1. Affordable Dining on Campus
- Capped Prices: Every caf√© in UNIMAS offers meals with prices capped under RM15, ensuring that students can have affordable dining options right on campus.

- Low-Cost Food Stalls: Many food stalls on campus sell meals for less than RM10, making it easy for students to find budget-friendly food choices.

2. Minimize Off-Campus Expenses
- Stay On-Campus: To save money, try to limit trips off-campus. UNIMAS offers a variety of amenities and services that cater to student needs, reducing the necessity to go elsewhere.

- Use E-Restaurant Services: Take advantage of e-restaurant services available on campus to order food conveniently and at a lower cost, avoiding the extra expenses associated with dining out.



By following these tips, you can effectively manage your budget and minimize expenses while studying at UNIMAS. Enjoy the convenience and affordability of campus life and make the most of the resources available to you.
