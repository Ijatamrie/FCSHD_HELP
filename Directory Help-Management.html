PESAKMA

The Cognitive Science and Human Development Student Association (PESAKMA) is one of the student bodies registered under the National Student Union (PKP) at the University Malaysia Sarawak (UNIMAS). They are students who have direct power to voice the opinions of students within the Faculty of Cognitive Science and Human Development (FCSHD).
