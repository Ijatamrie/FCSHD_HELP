Programme Outcome


Bachelor of Human Resource Development (Hons)
1. Understanding of HR Fundamentals: Students will acquire a comprehensive understanding of fundamental concepts in human resource development, including organizational behavior, management, and ICT applications.

2. Strategic Management Skills: They will develop strategic management skills essential for effective decision-making and leadership in HR contexts.

3. Competency in HR Practices: Students will gain practical competencies in various HR practices such as performance management, talent management, and conflict resolution.

4. Legal and Ethical Understanding: They will understand the legal and ethical frameworks governing HR practices, ensuring compliance and ethical conduct in organizational settings.

5. Research and Evaluation Skills: Students will develop research and evaluation skills necessary for analyzing HR practices and implementing evidence-based strategies.

Bachelor of Cognitive Science (Hons)
1. Foundational Knowledge: Students will acquire a strong foundation in cognitive science, encompassing areas such as cognitive psychology, neuroscience, and computer programming.

2. Analytical and Problem-Solving Skills: They will develop analytical and problem-solving skills crucial for understanding complex cognitive processes and developing solutions.

3. Application of Technology: Students will learn to apply technology effectively in cognitive science, including knowledge management, decision support systems, and artificial intelligence.

4. User Experience Design: They will gain expertise in user experience design, ensuring the optimization of technology interfaces for human cognition.

5. Leadership and Group Dynamics: Students will develop leadership and group dynamics skills essential for collaborative work in multidisciplinary cognitive science projects.

Bachelor of Counseling (Hons)
1. Counseling Theories and Skills: Students will gain a deep understanding of various counseling theories and develop essential counseling skills for working with individuals, groups, and families.

2. Psychological Understanding: They will develop a comprehensive understanding of psychological concepts and psychopathologies, enabling effective assessment and intervention.

3. Multicultural Competence: Students will acquire multicultural competence, essential for providing counseling services to diverse populations with sensitivity and respect.

4. Crisis Intervention Skills: They will develop skills in crisis intervention, enabling them to effectively support individuals experiencing acute psychological distress.

5. Research and Evidence-Based Practice: Students will learn research methods and evidence-based practices in counseling, ensuring the delivery of ethical and effective counseling services.

Bachelor of Psychology (Hons)
1. Foundational Knowledge in Psychology: Students will acquire a solid foundation in various fields of psychology, including social, developmental, abnormal, and cognitive psychology.

2. Clinical and Applied Skills: They will develop clinical and applied skills essential for conducting assessments, providing therapy, and implementing interventions in various psychological contexts.

3. Understanding of Human Behavior: Students will gain a deep understanding of human behavior, including factors influencing behavior in individual, social, and organizational settings.

4. Critical Thinking and Research Skills: They will develop critical thinking and research skills necessary for evaluating psychological theories, designing studies, and analyzing data.

5. Ethical Practice: Students will understand and adhere to ethical principles and guidelines in psychology, ensuring the ethical conduct of research and practice in psychology.

Programme Ideology

Bachelor of Human Resource Development (Hons)

Ideology: Developing Human Potential for Organizational Excellence

This program is centered on the ideology that effective human resource development is crucial for achieving organizational excellence. It emphasizes the importance of strategic management, ethical practices, and continuous learning and development to enhance the capabilities and performance of individuals within organizations.



Bachelor of Cognitive Science (Hons)

Ideology: Bridging Mind and Technology for Cognitive Advancement

The ideology behind this program is the integration of cognitive processes with technological advancements to improve human cognition and problem-solving abilities. It focuses on understanding the human mind and applying this knowledge to design better user experiences, support decision-making, and innovate in fields like artificial intelligence and neuroscience.



Bachelor of Counseling (Hons)

Ideology: Empowering Individuals Through Compassionate Counseling

This program is based on the ideology of empowering individuals by providing compassionate and effective counseling services. It emphasizes the development of core counseling skills, ethical practice, multicultural competence, and the ability to address psychological issues through evidence-based interventions.



Bachelor of Psychology (Hons)

Ideology: Understanding and Enhancing Human Behavior

The ideology of this program is to deepen the understanding of human behavior and apply psychological principles to enhance the well-being of individuals and communities. It focuses on comprehensive knowledge across various psychology fields, critical thinking, research skills, and ethical practice to address psychological challenges and promote mental health.
